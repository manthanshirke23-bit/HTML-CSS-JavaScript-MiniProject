Copper Kettle Brewing Co.

Marcus Rivera — Head Brewer
📍 42 Hopyard Lane, Downtown District 📅 Est. 2016 ⭐ 4.9 / 5.0 🍺 American Craft

Copper Kettle Brewing Co. was founded in 2016 by former chemical engineer Marcus Rivera, who turned his basement hobby into one of the city's most celebrated craft breweries. Located in a converted warehouse in the Downtown District, Copper Kettle is known for its bold IPAs, innovative seasonal releases, and a taproom that feels like a second living room. Their flagship Summer Haze IPA has won three consecutive regional gold medals.

Iron Forge Brewery

Lena Vandermeer — Head Brewer
📍 88 Furnace Street, Industrial Quarter 📅 Est. 2019 ⭐ 4.7 / 5.0 🍺 Belgian-American Craft

Iron Forge Brewery draws inspiration from the industrial history of its neighborhood. Founded by Belgian-born brewer Lena Vandermeer, the brewery specializes in Belgian-inspired ales with bold American character. The taproom features exposed steel beams, forge art installations, and 24 taps of creative, boundary-pushing beers.
